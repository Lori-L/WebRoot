Projects | Main

  • Kitty Combos (Will link to the final project website, once it has been completed)
    As our final project for ISIT 320, we in Human Systems worked together to create a website centered around Kitty Combos, a cat-themed tile-matching game similar to popular games like Bejeweled and Candy Crush.

    Upon the player starting the game, an 8x8 grid of random cat images is generated. Five cat images are pulled from a random cat image API, so players will see new images every time they play.

    Displayed here is an example of a randomly generated board.

    The player starts out with 15 turns and 0 points. Swapping two adjacent tiles to make a match decreases their turn counter, and making large tile combinations rewards the player with extra turns. Large tile combinations also give a higher number of points than the basic 3-tile combination, so the player is heavily incentivized to search for them.

    The player's main goal is to earn as many points as possible before running out of turns. Once this happens, they will be shown their final score and an online leaderboard that records and displays the game's top scores.
